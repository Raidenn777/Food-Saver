Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee to join Food Saver?

No, joining Food Saver is completely free for donors, recipients, and volunteers. We believe in removing barriers to food redistribution.

How do I know if my food is suitable for donation?

Edible food that is safe for consumption is generally suitable. We provide guidelines on food safety and handling to ensure all donations meet quality standards.

Can individuals join as donors?

Yes, absolutely! Individuals with surplus food from events, gatherings, or over-purchasing are welcome to join as donors.

What kind of time commitment is expected from volunteers?

We offer flexible volunteering opportunities. You can contribute as little as a few hours per month or as much as you'd like, based on your availability.

How are food donations collected and distributed?

We coordinate pickups from donors and deliveries to recipients. Our system matches donations based on location, quantity, and nutritional needs.
