About Food Saver

Food Saver is a digital platform that connects donors of surplus edible food—such as restaurants, hotels, and individuals—with NGOs, food banks, and those in need.

Our mission is to reduce food waste and fight hunger by redistributing excess food efficiently to those who need it most.

By bridging the gap between food surplus and food insecurity, we're creating a more sustainable and compassionate food system.
