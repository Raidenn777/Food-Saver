Our Core Objectives

At Food Saver, we are driven by a clear set of objectives that guide our mission to create a more sustainable and equitable food system

Reduce Food Waste

Divert edible food from landfills and reduce environmental impact through innovative redistribution channels.

30% of food wasted globally

Alleviate Hunger

Provide nutritious meals to food-insecure individuals and families by connecting surplus food with those in need.

828M people affected by hunger

Build Community

Create connections between donors, recipients, and volunteers to foster a sense of shared responsibility.

1000+ community partners

Track Impact

Measure and report on our collective environmental and social impact to continuously improve our effectiveness.

85% reduction in food waste

Strategic Goals

01

Expand Our Reach

Extend our services to 50+ cities within the next 5 years, establishing a nationwide network of food redistribution.

02

Enhance Technology

Develop AI-powered matching algorithms to optimize food redistribution based on nutritional needs, location, and expiration dates.

03

Educate & Advocate

Launch awareness campaigns to educate businesses and individuals about food waste reduction and hunger alleviation.

04

Build Partnerships

Forge strategic alliances with government agencies, corporations, and NGOs to amplify our impact.

How We Achieve Our Objectives

Technology-Driven Platform

Our user-friendly app and web platform make it easy for donors to list surplus food and for recipients to find what they need.

Efficient Logistics

We coordinate transportation and storage to ensure food reaches recipients while maintaining safety and quality standards.

Education & Training

We provide resources and training to help donors handle food safely and understand the impact of their contributions.
