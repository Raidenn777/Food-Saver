Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate food through Food Saver?

To donate food, simply register on our platform as a donor, list the surplus food you have available, and our system will match you with nearby recipients in need. You can arrange for pickup or delivery based on your convenience.

What types of food can I donate?

We accept all types of edible, non-perishable, and perishable food that is safe for consumption. This includes packaged goods, fresh produce, prepared meals, and more. All donations should meet food safety standards.

How do I become a recipient organization?

Non-profit organizations, NGOs, and community groups can register as recipients on our platform. After verification, you can browse available food donations and request what you need for your community.

Is there a cost to use the Food Saver platform?

No, our platform is completely free for both donors and recipients. We believe in removing barriers to food redistribution, so there are no hidden fees or charges for using our services.

How do you ensure food safety?

We have strict guidelines for food handling and safety. All donors are required to follow food safety protocols, and we provide educational resources to ensure proper handling of donated food.
